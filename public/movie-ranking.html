3 Movies watched and enjoyed

Deadpool & Wolverine

Deadpool and Wolverine are two of the most popular characters in the Marvel Universe. Deadpool is known for his sarcastic humor and Wolverine is known for his healing factor and adamantium claws. Both characters have appeared in multiple movies and comic books.

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is a biographical drama film directed by Christopher Nolan. The film tells the story of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the American theoretical physicist who played a key role in the development of the atomic bomb during World War II. The film stars Cillian Murphy in the lead role.

Furiosa

Furiosa is an action film directed by George Miller. The film is a prequel to the 2015 film Mad Max: Fury Road and focuses on the character Imperator Furiosa, played by Anya Taylor-Joy. The film is set to explore the origins of the character and her journey to becoming a warrior.
